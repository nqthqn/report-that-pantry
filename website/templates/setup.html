How To Get Your Pantry Status Setup

1. First, head over to the Add Organization page, where you need to enter your organizations name and address.

2. Next, you will have to create an account under the Sign Up Page, and enter in all the required information.

3. Then, head down to the Add Locations page, and enter in the required information for each of your pantries.

4. Lastly, for each pantry you add, you will recevice a poster like the one below to print out and put on that pantry.
